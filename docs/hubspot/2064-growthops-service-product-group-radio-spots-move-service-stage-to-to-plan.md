*   **Workflow Name:** 2064 | GrowthOps | Service | Product Group = Radio Spots | Move Service Stage to “To Plan”

### **Purpose**

This workflow automates the initial stage management for Service objects. When a service is identified as being for "Radio Spots," this workflow automatically moves it into the "To Plan" stage of the service pipeline, ensuring it is immediately ready for the planning team.

### **Trigger / Enrollment Criteria**

This workflow enrolls Service objects that meet the following condition:

*   The **Product Group** property is equal to **Radio Spots**.

_Note: Objects will only be enrolled once and will not re-enroll if they meet the criteria again._

### **Actions**

Once a Service object is enrolled, the workflow performs the following action:

*   **Set Property Value:** Changes the **Pipeline Stage** of the Service object to **To Plan**.
